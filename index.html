The Impact of Tech Education

In The Gambia, access to digital skills and programming is growing. As seen in the image, students are engaged in learning, using laptops, projectors, and discussions to expand their knowledge. This tribute honors the dedication of teachers, mentors, and students who believe in the power of education to change lives.

A Community of Learners

The presence of a diverse group of learners in this classroom highlights the importance of collaboration. Whether young or old, every individual has the potential to create something meaningful through education. The commitment of mentors and trainers to guide students on the path of success is commendable.

Timeline of Technology Education in The Gambia

  • 2000s: Introduction of basic ICT education in schools.
  • 2010: First major tech training initiatives launched by NGOs and private institutions.
  • 2015: Expansion of internet access, making online learning more accessible.
  • 2018: Government collaborates with tech organizations to promote digital literacy.
  • 2020: Remote learning and coding boot camps become popular.
  • 2023-Present: More universities and institutions offer specialized IT and programming courses.

A Call to Action

This tribute serves as a reminder that technology and education can transform lives. By investing in learning, sharing knowledge, and encouraging innovation, we can build a brighter future for The Gambia and beyond.
